Rules of Human vs Computer

You will be playing against the computer

There will be 5 rounds in total

You will be able to pick between 3 options of Rock, Paper and Scissors.

The first player to reach 5 points will win.

Here are the rules for getting a point

  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Paper beats Rock
  • Scissors beats Paper

If the player and computer both choose the same option (e.g. Rock and Rock etc.), then no one will get the point.

Score: 0 of 5
Player Score: 0
Computer Score: 0
